The Countertop Connection is a small company out of Uxbridge, MA, and a family friend. They sought me out during my first year of college to update their website to look more modern and hopefully draw in more customers.

The goal of creating this site was to keep the same general style and copy as the site before it. They wanted a logo, nav bar, and a bunch of images across the site. But, when all was said and done they had a few more ideas for me to implement. We added social media, links to the granite manufacturers, and a page for them to display new granite colors as they came out.

ZOMG Clan has been a project for my friends and I since the middle of highschool.

It initially started out as a gaming clan for CS:S, but eventually spiralled into a site, then a blog, and finally into social media. I was generally the manager for site development and brand development on social media, although within the past year or so our efforts have died down.

The Facebook is still run by Pepi Simeonov, but until we have more time as a group to gather our thoughts, the site will remain offline. And unfortunately I do not have pictures of any 'pretty' designs within the past few years. And sadly the code from high school is not something I want to show off.
